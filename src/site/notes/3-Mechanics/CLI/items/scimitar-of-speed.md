# Scimitar of Speed
*Weapon ([scimitar](3-Mechanics/CLI/items/scimitar.md)), major, very rare (requires attunement)*  


- **Damage**: 1d6 slashing
- **Properties**: [Finesse](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/item-properties.md#Finesse), [Light](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/item-properties.md#Light)
- **Weight**: 3.0 lbs.

You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. In addition, you can make one attack with it as a bonus action on each of your turns.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide p. 199. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.1)'>SRD</span>*
